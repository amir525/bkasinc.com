Privacy Policy

BKAS INC is solely meant for maintaining a relationship with its users, on the basis of their personal identity and information, by promoting practices of fair information usage. This privacy policy covers all online properties maintained and owned by BKAS INC. As BKAS INC is committed to its users’ policy, we disclose:

If users have queries or doubts regarding this statement, contact BKAS INC customer support by email or phone.

Information Collection

BKAS INC is the only owner of the information collected on its web, including www.bkasinc.com. BKAS INC ask you for your personal information at different points on our website for enhanced online experience with us.

Registration

For using BKAS INC bookkeeping services, and accessing certain areas of this website, you may be required to fill a registration form. For registration, you are required to give your contact information, like name, phone number and email address. We use your contact information to contact you regarding products and services on our sites. You have the choice of giving your demographic information; it helps us to improve your online experience on our site.

Profile

The information that we collect through cookies and log files is used to set up a profile of you. This profile stores information about the viewing preferences of users on our website. Further, information thus collected is linked to user’s personally identifiable information in order to improve the content of our site for users. Also, this profile helps us to customize a user’s visit to our website and direct them directly to relevant promotions for them.

We prohibit the sharing of personally identifiable elements of your profile with other third parties. Your profile is only shared in aggregate form by us.

Order

When you place an order on our site, we request your information to purchase through order form. In this form, you must submit your accurate contact information, like name, email and billing address and financial details including your credit card number and its expiration date. This helps us in fulfilling our customer’s orders and for creating billing details.

Log Files

Like most Web sites, our servers use log files to analyze trends, administer the site, track user’s movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Cookies

Cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer linked to information about the user. In no means usage of cookies on our site is linked to any personally identifiable information. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. In case of session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. On the other hand, persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user’s hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed with the help of Internet browser help file directions. With the help of persistent cookies, we are able to enrich user’s experience on this site.

Clear Gifs

Clear gifs are tiny graphics with a unique identifier, similar in function to cookies, and are used to track the online movements of Web users. We use clear gifs in our HTML-based emails to let us know which of our emails are opened by our recipients. This helps us to analyze the effectiveness of certain communications and the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. If users wish to opt-out of these emails, please see our Opt-out section.

Children’s Privacy

We do not deliberately collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If we ever come to know that we have personal information on a child under the age of 16, we will delete that information from our systems. BKAS INC encourages parents to go online with their kids. Here are a few tips to help make a child’s online experience safer:

Make your children understand that they should never give their personal details to anyone unless supervised by parent or any other responsible adult. As a parent know what all sites your child visit and how these sites collect information from kids.

Safeguards and Data Retention

User Information

BKAS INC takes every precaution to protect its user’s information. When users submit sensitive information via BKAS INC Website, their information is protected both online and off-line.

When our registration/order forms ask users to enter sensitive information (such as credit card number), that information is encrypted and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry.

While we use to protect sensitive information online, we also rely on security measures to protect user-information off-line. All of our users’ information, not just the sensitive information mentioned above, is restricted in our offices. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, our billing clerks or a customer service representative) are granted access to personally identifiable information. Finally, the servers that store personally identifiable information are in a secure environment.

Message Content Handling

We will store your personal Transmissions on our servers for a limited time and will endeavor to purge old transmissions from its servers when the expiration period has run out (up to 365 days).We use industry-standard efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of your transmissions (as well as your personally identifiable information). These are the same technologies that most websites use to safeguard credit card information and other personal information. However, that said, “perfect security” does not exist over telephone lines and the Internet and BKAS INC accordingly accepts only limited liability for security breaches.

Data Retention

The time period for which we keep information varies according to what the information is used for. In some cases, there are legal requirements to keep data for a minimum period. Unless there is a specific legal requirement for us to keep the information, we will retain it for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the data was collected or for which it is to be further processed.
